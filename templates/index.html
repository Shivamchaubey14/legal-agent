What LexAI does

Everything a first-pass review needs,
automated

Not a chatbot. An autonomous agent that reads, reasons, and reports.

Autonomous Clause Detection
LangGraph agent reads every chunk of your contract and flags Termination, Liability, IP, Payment, Confidentiality, Non-compete, and Governing Law clauses automatically.
Risk Scoring
Each clause gets a Low / Medium / High risk level. A weighted formula produces an overall contract score: Low, Medium, High, or Critical.
Redline Suggestions
Every risky clause comes with a suggested rewrite grounded in standard legal playbooks. A diff view shows exactly what changed in red and green.
Q&A with Citations
Ask anything about your contract. The agent retrieves the relevant clauses and playbook context, then answers with exact clause numbers and page references.
Downloadable Report
Generate a professional PDF review report covering risk summary, all flagged clauses, suggested rewrites, and an executive summary — ready to share.
RAG-Grounded Advice
Answers are grounded in a ChromaDB vector store of real legal playbooks — NDA, SaaS, employment, lease, vendor — not hallucinated legal advice.
How it works

Three steps to a complete review

Upload your contract
Drop in a PDF or DOCX — up to 50 pages. LexAI extracts text with PyMuPDF and falls back to Tesseract OCR for scanned documents. Your file is stored securely and only you can access it.
